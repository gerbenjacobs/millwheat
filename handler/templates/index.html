Millwheat is a PBBG set in medieval times.

You have inherited your parents title as ruler of the town. It's not much of a town, but with some determination and elbow grease you feel confident you can change that.

What will you focus on? Heavy mining? Maybe set up a big forestry and logging town, or perhaps you prefer to set up the town for making end products like armour, weaponry and pottery.

Clearly you need to upgrade your town and get ready to one day rule the country.
